Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 19, 2026 · Publisher: Point Zero AI LLC · App: Owner Pulse (iOS)

Owner Pulse is operated by Point Zero AI LLC. Owner Pulse helps business owners track daily operating gaps and reminder preferences.

Plain English: This version of Owner Pulse saves your daily check-in entries on your phone, not on our servers. We don't sell or share your information.

Information stored in the app

This version may save Daily Pulse entries, note text, assessment status, selected top gap, and morning/evening reminder times on the user's device.

Account and sync

This version does not create an Owner Pulse account and does not sync Daily Pulse entries to a Point Zero AI server.

Point Zero Assessment

Owner Pulse may open the Point Zero AI assessment website. Assessment results are delivered through the assessment experience and email flow, not directly imported into the app. If the user records a top gap inside Owner Pulse, that selection is saved on device.

Notifications

If the user allows notifications, Owner Pulse may schedule local morning and evening reminders at the times chosen by the user. Notifications can be disabled in iOS Settings.

Premium Preview

Premium screens may show demo data to explain planned calendar and Google Business Profile signal features. Demo ratings, counts, and priorities are illustrative only and are not financial, medical, legal, credit, or valuation scoring.

We do not sell data

Point Zero AI LLC does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-party analytics

The current app does not include third-party advertising tracking, third-party analytics SDKs, or third-party crash-reporting SDKs. If that changes, the policy and App Store privacy disclosures must be updated before release.

Business and financial disclaimer

Owner Pulse is an operating check-in tool. It does not provide financial advice, legal advice, credit scoring, business valuation, revenue estimates, or guaranteed business outcomes.

Children's privacy

Owner Pulse is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact

Questions or concerns should go through the Owner Pulse support page.
