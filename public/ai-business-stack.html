The Reach

400 clients. Face to face. 2 hours.

$59 a month.

I had a subset of 400 clients in my retirement business I needed to contact. Reaching all of them personally — real message, real face, real conversation — used to mean days of calls and emails, or a mass blast that nobody reads.

Instead I used Dubb. In under two hours, I filmed, edited, and sent personalized video messages to all 400 of them. They opened their email and saw my face — not a template. Me, talking directly to them.

Then I used Tella — $39 a month — to record instruction videos where I'm presenting data on one side of the screen while they can see me on the other. Walking clients through 401(k) fee disclosures, visually, personally. What used to require an in-person meeting or a production crew now takes an afternoon.
