SMS Terms & Conditions

Text message program terms for Point Zero AI LLC

Effective Date: April 23, 2026  ·  Point Zero AI LLC  ·  Arizona, USA

Program Description

Point Zero AI LLC ("Point Zero AI," "we," "us," or "our") operates a text message (SMS) program to deliver assessment results, strategy call reminders, follow-up communications, and educational content to users who have voluntarily provided their mobile phone number and given express consent by opting in.

This program is operated in compliance with The Campaign Registry (TCR) and applicable mobile carrier guidelines (AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, and others). Messages are delivered via our messaging partner, GoHighLevel (LeadConnector), using the Twilio carrier network.

How You Opt In

You opt in to receive text messages from Point Zero AI by taking both of the following actions on one of our forms (such as the one on our AI Business Assessment page):

  • Voluntarily entering your mobile phone number
  • Checking the dedicated SMS consent checkbox that states you agree to receive text messages from Point Zero AI

Your phone number is never added to our SMS program without both steps. SMS opt-in is separate from and not required for any other service on our website — you can complete the assessment, receive results by email, and purchase products without ever opting in to SMS.

What You'll Receive

  • Notifications when your AI Business Assessment results are ready or updated
  • Reminders for strategy calls or appointments you've scheduled with Tom Zgainer or Point Zero AI
  • Follow-up messages related to services or products you've inquired about
  • Occasional educational content, product updates, and relevant business insights from Point Zero AI

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your engagement with our services. You will receive up to 4 messages per month under normal circumstances. Transactional messages (such as appointment confirmations you've requested) do not count toward this limit.

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier messaging rates from your mobile provider will apply to each message you send or receive. Point Zero AI does not charge you any fee for messages — all costs are determined by your wireless carrier's plan. Contact your carrier for details about your specific rates.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message from Point Zero AI at any time to immediately unsubscribe from all text messages.

After replying STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message, and no further SMS messages will be sent to your number.

You may also email us at support@pointzeroai.com to request opt-out, and we will remove your number from our SMS program promptly.

How to Get Help

Reply HELP to any message from Point Zero AI to receive customer support information, or email us directly at support@pointzeroai.com.

Supported Carriers

This SMS program is available on all major U.S. wireless carriers, including but not limited to AT&T, Verizon Wireless, T-Mobile, Sprint, U.S. Cellular, Boost Mobile, MetroPCS, Cricket Wireless, and other participating carriers. Service may not be available on all carriers at all times.

Carrier Non-Liability

Wireless carriers (including but not limited to AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, U.S. Cellular, and others) are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery of messages is subject to effective transmission by your wireless carrier and is not guaranteed.

Privacy & Data Handling

Your mobile phone number and SMS consent record are handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Specifically:

  • We never sell, rent, lease, or share your phone number with third parties for their marketing purposes.
  • Your phone number is stored securely and used solely to deliver the SMS messages you have opted into.
  • Data shared with our messaging partner (GoHighLevel / Twilio) is strictly for the purpose of delivering messages and is handled under their respective privacy and data-protection standards.
  • When you opt out, your phone number is flagged in our system so no further messages are sent, in accordance with carrier compliance rules.

Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years of age and the account holder or authorized user of the mobile number you provide in order to enroll in the SMS program. By opting in, you represent and warrant that you meet these eligibility requirements.

Changes to These Terms

We may update these SMS Terms & Conditions from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. Material changes to the program will be communicated to enrolled users via text message or email where practicable.
