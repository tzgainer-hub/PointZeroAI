The Five Daily Gaps

Five operating gaps. Checked every day.

These are the recurring leaks that quietly cost attention in most owner-led businesses. My Business Pulse turns them into a daily rhythm instead of a once-a-quarter scramble.

1

Lead response

The new inquiries waiting on a callback or a reply right now. Speed is the gap most owners don't realize they're losing.

2

Quote follow-up

The estimates already sent that have gone quiet. Revenue you already earned the right to ask for again.

3

Past clients

The buyers who already know you. A 2-minute touch reopens work most owners never go back for.

4

Review requests

The asks that compound over months. One a day beats a quarterly review campaign every single time.

5

Owner bottlenecks

The decisions only you can make. My Business Pulse keeps them visible so the team isn't stuck waiting on a yes.
