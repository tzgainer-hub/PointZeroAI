The Five Daily Gaps

Five operating gaps. Checked every day.

These are the recurring leaks that quietly cost attention in most owner-led businesses. My Business Pulse turns them into a daily rhythm instead of a once-a-quarter scramble.

1

Lead response

The new inquiries waiting on a callback or a reply right now. Speed is the gap most owners don't realize they're losing.

2

Quote follow-up

The estimates already sent that have gone quiet. Revenue you already earned the right to ask for again.

3

Past clients

The buyers who already know you. A 2-minute touch reopens work most owners never go back for.

4

Review requests

The asks that compound over months. One a day beats a quarterly review campaign every single time.

5

Owner bottlenecks

The decisions only you can make. My Business Pulse keeps them visible so the team isn't stuck waiting on a yes.

Inside My Business Pulse

The actual screens.

The first screen on a fresh install, the daily plan, the evening close, and the Premium money tools.

Premium

Premium turns the gaps into recoverable owner actions.

Essential finds the gaps. Premium turns each one into a concrete owner move you can take today, with everything kept on device.

1

Quote Recovery

Add open quotes with the actual quote amount. See total open quote value, oldest quote, status, and follow-up visibility at a glance. Values come only from what you enter.

2

Review Ask System

Log each completed job by name, then mark each one Review Asked and Review Received as the loop closes. No Google or Yelp connection required — just a visible habit for the asks that compound over months.

3

Calendar Pulse

With your permission, Premium scans your calendar on device to surface quote follow-ups, review asks, past-client reactivation, and the next move per appointment. Events are processed on device and never uploaded.

4

Script Bank

Plain-language scripts ready to copy and send: quote follow-up, review ask, missed-call return, past-client reach-out. Written for owners, not marketers.

5

Weekly Owner Brief

An operating checklist built from your app entries: open quote value, oldest quote, review-ask gap, calendar moves, and the next owner moves for the week.
