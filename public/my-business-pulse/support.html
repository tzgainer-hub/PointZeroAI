Frequently asked

What is My Business Pulse?

My Business Pulse is an app that helps business owners create a daily rhythm around five operating gaps: lead response, quote follow-up, past clients, review requests, and owner bottlenecks.

How do I use My Business Pulse?

Use Morning Pulse to set the day's priorities. Use Evening Pulse to record what stayed open. The Home screen turns saved entries into a simple 7-day picture.

How does the score work?

The Evening Pulse score is based only on saved answers inside the app. For each of the five gaps, None = 0, 1–2 = 1, and 3+ = 3. Lower gap totals produce a higher pulse score.

Is My Business Pulse financial advice?

No. My Business Pulse is an operating check-in tool. It does not provide financial advice, legal advice, credit scoring, business valuation, revenue estimates, or guarantees.

Where are my entries saved?

This version saves Daily Pulse entries, notes, assessment status, selected top gap, and reminder times on the user's device. It does not create an account or sync entries to a Point Zero AI server.

How do reminders work?

If notifications are allowed, My Business Pulse can send morning and evening reminders at the times chosen in Settings. Users can also manage notification permission in iOS Settings.
