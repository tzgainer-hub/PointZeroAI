The Founder

I've done this before.

I spent a large part of my career building a company that explained something complicated — retirement plans — in plain English. I helped over 8,000 businesses reduce their fees and saved working families more than a billion dollars collectively. Tony Robbins put the work in two of his bestsellers. Eventually I sold to one of the largest wealth management firms in the country.

These days I build small software. One app at a time. No agency, no client roster, no team to manage. Just the part of building I actually enjoy: finding a real problem and making a simple, well-made tool that solves it. It keeps the mind sharp, and it is quietly satisfying in a way the big, loud stuff never was.

That is what Point Zero AI is now. A studio of one, building in the open, on its own schedule.
